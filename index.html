Welcome to TNAP

The Never Alone Project The Never Alone Project aims to support bereaved children and their families in the UK online. We also offer face to face services in Northamptonshire. We stand together as a community to reduce isolation and create a safe space to deal with grief..

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to provide unparalleled support to those dealing with grief, creating a community of hope and resilience.

Current Campaign: Wellingborough Music Festival

Join us at the Wellingborough Music Festival and our Christmas Tree Campaign to support grieving children and families.

Why We Do What We Do

We receive 50 new referrals a month. A child loses a parent every 30 minutes. These numbers highlight the critical need for our work.
